About WatchBox

Tired of endlessly scrolling through streaming platforms, wondering “What should I watch next?” or “Did I already watch that one?” Well, say hello to WatchBox!

With WatchBox, you can:

  • - Track Your Viewing Habits: Log every TV show, movie, or docuseries you've watched.
  • - Create a Watchlist: Got an endless list of “I'll watch this later” recommendations? Now it's easy to remember what you've been meaning to watch for months.
  • - Rate your shows: Keep track of what you've enjoyed and what you haven't!
